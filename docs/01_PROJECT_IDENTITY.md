# Beyond TULIP v2 Blueprint

## 01. Project Identity

### Purpose

Beyond TULIP exists to encourage believers to examine every doctrine in the light of Scripture. The project seeks to present a Scripture-first understanding of the gospel through careful biblical exegesis, historical theology, and respectful theological dialogue.

### Vision

To become a trusted online theological library that equips Christians to understand, evaluate, and defend biblical doctrine with confidence, humility, and faithfulness to God's Word.

### Mission

The mission of Beyond TULIP is to:

- Exalt Jesus Christ.
- Uphold the authority of Scripture.
- Explain the gospel clearly.
- Examine Calvinist theology fairly.
- Encourage careful Bible study.
- Equip believers for apologetics.
- Present theology with charity, accuracy, and intellectual honesty.

### Core Principle

Scripture is the highest authority.

Every doctrine, tradition, theological system, and human teacher must ultimately be tested by the Word of God.
## Target Audience

Beyond TULIP is written primarily for Christians who desire to study Scripture carefully and evaluate theological doctrines in light of God's Word. While the project focuses on examining Calvinist theology, it is intended to be accessible to believers from a wide range of evangelical backgrounds, as well as to anyone sincerely seeking to understand the biblical doctrine of salvation.

## What Beyond TULIP Is

Beyond TULIP is a Scripture-first theological library dedicated to examining Calvinist theology in the light of Scripture.

The project evaluates theological doctrines through careful biblical exegesis, historical theology, and respectful theological dialogue.

It argues that several central doctrines of Calvinist soteriology are inconsistent with the teaching of Scripture and seeks to present a clear, biblically grounded understanding of the gospel of Jesus Christ.

## What Beyond TULIP Is Not

Beyond TULIP is not a denominational ministry.

It is not a church.

It is not a debate platform intended to generate controversy.

It does not seek to attack individual Christians or question their sincerity, faith, or love for Christ.

Rather, it seeks to examine theological doctrines in the light of Scripture and to encourage believers to search the Scriptures for themselves.

Our desire is not to promote division but to promote biblical truth, theological clarity, and a deeper understanding of the gospel of Jesus Christ.
## Long-Term Goals

The long-term vision for Beyond TULIP is to become a comprehensive, Scripture-first theological library that serves Christians around the world.

The project aims to:

- Build a comprehensive theological library centered on Scripture.
- Present clear and biblically grounded articles on salvation, Calvinism, and related doctrines.
- Develop a complete Bible commentary emphasizing sound biblical exegesis.
- Create theological diagrams, comparison charts, and educational resources.
- Equip Christians with reliable apologetics resources.
- Maintain a professional, accessible, and well-organized website that continues to grow for many years.
- Glorify Jesus Christ by encouraging believers to search the Scriptures and to test every doctrine by God's Word.
