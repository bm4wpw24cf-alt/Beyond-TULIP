# 12. Quality Assurance Standard

## Purpose

This document defines the quality assurance process for all content published within the Beyond TULIP project.

Its purpose is to ensure that every article, Bible commentary, theological analysis, and educational resource meets the project's standards for biblical accuracy, theological integrity, clarity, and consistency before publication.

---

## Primary Principle

Accuracy takes precedence over speed.

Every publication should be reviewed before it is considered complete.

---

## Quality Objectives

Every publication should be:

- Biblically accurate
- Contextually faithful
- Theologically consistent
- Historically responsible
- Clearly written
- Logically organized
- Properly sourced

---

## Review Process

Before publication, every document should be reviewed for:

1. Biblical accuracy
2. Exegetical consistency
3. Historical accuracy
4. Theological consistency
5. Logical structure
6. Language and grammar
7. Citation accuracy
8. Formatting consistency

---

## Biblical Review

Confirm that:

- Scripture is interpreted within context.
- Cross-references are appropriate.
- No doctrine depends upon isolated proof texts.
- Conclusions follow from the biblical evidence.

---

## Historical Review

Verify:

- Historical claims.
- Dates.
- People.
- Events.
- Cultural background.

Avoid unsupported historical assertions.

---

## Theological Review

Ensure that:

- Opposing views are represented fairly.
- Arguments are evaluated rather than caricatured.
- Conclusions distinguish between biblical teaching and theological inference.

---

## Source Review

Confirm that:

- Sources are reliable.
- Quotations are accurate.
- References are complete.
- Primary sources are preferred whenever possible.

---

## Writing Review

Check for:

- Clear organization.
- Consistent terminology.
- Readability.
- Concise explanations.
- Smooth transitions.

Avoid unnecessary repetition.

---

## AI Self-Review

Before finalizing content, the AI should ask:

- Have I interpreted the biblical text fairly?
- Have I represented opposing views accurately?
- Have I distinguished facts from interpretations?
- Have I supported my conclusions with evidence?
- Have I acknowledged uncertainty where appropriate?

---

## Publication Checklist

Before publication, confirm:

- Biblical accuracy
- Historical accuracy
- Logical consistency
- Citation accuracy
- Language quality
- Formatting consistency

Every item should be completed before publication.

---

## AI Expectations

The AI should prioritize:

- Accuracy over speed
- Evidence over assertion
- Clarity over complexity
- Fairness over polemics
- Scripture over tradition

---

## Final Principle

Every published document should reflect careful scholarship, intellectual honesty, and faithfulness to Scripture.

> **Publish only what you are prepared to defend from Scripture.**
