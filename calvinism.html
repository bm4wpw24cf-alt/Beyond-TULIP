Examining Calvinism

Understanding Calvinism through Scripture, history, and theological sources.

Calvinism has profoundly influenced Protestant theology for centuries. The purpose of this section is to explain Calvinist doctrine as Calvinists themselves understand it. Each article presents the historical background, biblical foundations, and theological reasoning of the Reformed tradition in a fair and accurate manner, helping readers understand the system on its own terms.
